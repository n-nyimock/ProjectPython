Microsoft Word

You can create all types of official documents in Microsoft Word. Distraction-free reading is a more specialized feature available in Word 2010 onwards. Though primarily designed for touch-enabled tablets, the Read Mode works well on an everyday laptop as well. To access it quickly, press Alt, followed by W + F simultaneously
